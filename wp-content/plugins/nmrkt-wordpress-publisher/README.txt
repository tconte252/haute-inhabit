== Description ==

The NMRKT publisher Wordpress plugin will integrate your shop inventory management into your blogging workflow. It will help you search for
the products you want to write about, insert images and links to your posts, and automatically activate items into your shop.

To use this plugin you will require a NMRKT publisher account.

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Upload `nmrkt-wordpress-publisher` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Visit the Settings > NMRKT page and input your NMRKT account ID and Secret to activate! Your NMRKT account settings can be found
on the home page of the NMRKT dashboard: http://market.nmrkt.com

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I really need a NMRKT account? =

Yes. You have to have a PUBLISHER account with NMRKT to use this plugin. It is meant to sync with your shop inventory and give you the ability
to add product and photos directly to your posts.

= Where do I sign up? =

http://nmrkt.com

== Screenshots ==

1. Once installed correctly, the plugin will give you a NMRKT tab in your "Add Media" console inside any post. Here you can search your NMRKT inventory for items that you want to write about.
2. The plugin will show you all matches to your search. You can select the item you want to add.
3. Then choose the options on the right and the plugin will add the HTML to your post.
